Privacy Policy

DailyDose

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

DailyDose is a simple, privacy-first iOS application designed to help you manage medications and vitamins through local reminders and inventory tracking.

Privacy Policy Terms of Service

1. Information We Collect

All data you enter — medication names, schedules, dosages, quantities, notes, colors, forms, start dates, and active status — is stored exclusively on your device using SwiftData.

We collect no personal information.

We do not collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • HealthKit data
  • Analytics
  • Usage statistics
  • Any information that could identify you

2. How We Use Information

Your data stays only on your device and is used solely to:

  • Display your medication list
  • Schedule and deliver local notifications via UserNotifications
  • Track inventory and show refill alerts
  • Allow pausing/resuming or editing entries

No data ever leaves your device.

3. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not share, sell, transmit, or disclose your data to anyone — including us (there is no "us" server), third parties, analytics providers, advertisers, or any external service.

4. Data Storage & Security

All information remains locally on your iOS device.

Security relies on Apple's standard device protections (encryption at rest, sandboxing, etc.).

We have no access to your data and cannot recover it if your device is lost, reset, or damaged.

5. Children's Privacy

DailyDose is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy (unlikely, given the app's design), the new version will be included in a future app release. Continued use after an update constitutes acceptance.

7. Contact

Questions? Reach out via the app's support link or the GitHub repository issues.

In three words: Local. Private. Forever.

No tracking, no servers, no surprises — just your data on your phone.
