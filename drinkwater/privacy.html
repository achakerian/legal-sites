Privacy Policy

DrinkWater App

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

DrinkWater App is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our water tracking application.

Privacy Policy Terms of Service

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

DrinkWater App stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Water consumption logs — Daily records of water intake amounts
  • Goal settings — Your daily water consumption goal and tap amount preferences
  • Schedule preferences — Wake and sleep times for notification scheduling
  • Streak data — Consecutive days of meeting your hydration goal

This data never leaves your device. We do not have access to it, and it is not transmitted to any servers.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Health data beyond what you manually log in the app
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Any data to third-party services

How We Use Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Display your water consumption progress
  • Track your hydration streaks
  • Send local notifications based on your schedule preferences
  • Sync data between the app and home screen widget (on your device only)

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Data is shared between the main app and widget using Apple's App Groups feature, which keeps data sandboxed on your device.

We do not use cloud storage, external databases, or any form of data backup to remote servers.

Notifications

DrinkWater App may send local push notifications to remind you to stay hydrated. These notifications are:

  • Generated entirely on your device
  • Based on your configured wake/sleep schedule
  • Controllable through iOS Settings

No notification data is sent to external servers.

Children's Privacy

DrinkWater App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app stores all data locally and does not require account creation or personal information input.

Third-Party Services

DrinkWater App does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Deleting the app will remove all associated data
  • You can reset your progress at any time using the reset button in the app
  • No data persists on external servers after app deletion

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information for us to provide, modify, or delete. All your data remains under your control on your device.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the app's support link or GitHub repository.
