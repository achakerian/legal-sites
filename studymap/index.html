About StudyMap

StudyMap is a privacy-focused academic planner for university students. All your data stays on your iPhone — no accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking.

  • Manage units and assessments across your degree
  • Enter marks and view real-time grade calculations
  • Visualize workload with a monthly heatmap
  • Review course history organized by year and session
  • Support for multiple grading scales (AU, US, UK)
  • Recurring assessments with "Best Of" aggregation
